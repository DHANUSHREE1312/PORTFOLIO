About me.

Hi! I'm Mironshoh, a frontend developer who sees programming not just
as work but as a form of art that blends creativity and technology.

I’m currently studying at IDU University in Tashkent, where I’m deepening my IT knowledge
and gaining hands-on experience through practical projects. My journey into web development
began with a passion for creating modern, user-friendly, and visually appealing websites.

Over time, that passion grew into a professional skill — now I build official, non-official,
and creative user interfaces using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript . I pay strong attention to code
clarity, structure, and performance optimization in every project I work on. For me, one of the
most important aspects of web development is user experience (UX) — because a website should not
only look beautiful but also feel smooth and intuitive to use.

So far, I’ve worked on several small and medium-scale personal and team projects, including small-page
websites, official school and boarding school sites, and various creative mini-projects . Each one
has helped me gain new skills, technical insight, and a better understanding of how to turn design into function.

Beyond coding, I enjoy exploring new technologies, following design trends, and contributing to open-source projects.

My ultimate goal is to grow into a skilled frontend specialist who creates digital products that are both
useful and aesthetically pleasing.

If you're looking for a passionate, detail-oriented, and creative frontend developer — feel free
to get in touch!

My skills.

I’m a frontend developer skilled in modern web technologies.
With CSS, I create scroll animations, hover transitions, and
responsive layouts that enhance user experience.

In JavaScript, I work with Promises and async/await to control
loading effects, data fetching, and smooth animations.

I enjoy making every UI element move naturally, using techniques
like Intersection Observer, setTimeout, and transition-delay to
reveal elements smoothly while scrolling.

Currently, I’m learning TypeScript and React, and exploring
how to fetch and manage real-time data using the Fetch API.

My goal is to combine beautiful design with technical excellence,
building fast, responsive, and professional frontend solutions.
